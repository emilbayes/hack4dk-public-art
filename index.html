Gender Distribution in Public Art

The graphic below shows the distribution of public art in Denmark, as bought by Statens Kunstfond. The teal area is male artists, the redorange area is female artists. The x-axis shows the creation year of the piece, with some uncertainty. The dark curve shows the difference between male and female artists, meaning a curvature around zero would mean a "fair" distribution. Each of the vertical, white lines show when a new committee have started their period.
